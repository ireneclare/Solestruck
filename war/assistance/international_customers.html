International Customers

Shipping

Free standard shipping on all international orders over $199.00 USD!

  • Orders with standard shipping will be shipped by USPS Priority International (United States Postal Service). See below for estimated arrival times.
  • Express orders are shipped by UPS or FedEx.
  • Customer must pay any customs, duties and/or taxes at delivery of the package.
  • If any package is refused or undeliverable, customer will be responsible for any additional fees and the original shipping cost is non-refundable.

Payments:

  • Visa, MasterCard, Discover, American Express, any Bankcard with the Visa or MasterCard logo and Paypal (please instant email us to pay with Paypal).

Returns:

Due to the high cost of shipping, Solestruck does not provide returns on international orders. Customers must make their own accommodations for any returns. Any product you wish to return must be sent in its original packaging and in unworn condition. We cannot accept returns for used merchandise. Please note that the original shipping charge of your order is non-refundable. If you don't see your country listed below, please instant email us at customerservice@ solestruck.com.
