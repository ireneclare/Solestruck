Returns

Why everyone loves Solestruck: We're an independent, family-run boutique store. We run with a small team of obsessed staff that treats you like you just walked in to your favorite local small town store. We carry unique and youth-driven brands to keep you looking fresh and fashion forward. Our goal is to rid the world of ugly shoes one pair at a time. We've been doing it for five years now. Here, you can werk it.. and werk it good.

We like to keep things simple and easy around here:

  • Returns are only $5.95 per prepaid return label used and we just deduct it from your refund to make it easy on you (for US addresses only).
  • Life happens so you have a full 60 days from the original purchase date to return a purchase. All shoes must be returned in brand new condition.
  • International customers click here for more information.

Returning shoes is a sad moment. Say goodbye then just follow these easy steps:

  1. All shoes must be returned in brand new condition. Please try shoes on a carpeted surface to avoid any damage. Sorry, but we cannot accept returns for used, dirty or damaged shoes and these will be returned at customer's expense.
  2. Fill out the bottom portion of the shipping invoice.
  3. Place the shoebox inside the shipping box (must be returned inside a shipping box to protect the shoe box).
  4. Login to your accountto download your prepaid UPS return label or instantly email us for a label if you don't have an account. Can't use a UPS label? Just return them at your convenience and we won't charge the return fee.
  5. Drop them off at any UPS location, drop box or with a driver. It will take up to five business days to arrive back to us.
  6. We'll get your return processed within 48 hours of receiving it, which will be a refund for the cost of the shoes less the $5.95 return fee. You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed. We cannot refund any original shipping costs.
  7. Then you should see the credit back to the original form of payment used in 3.5 business days, depending on how fast your bank is.

I want to exchange them!

No problem. You can write on the return form what different size or color you would like to exchange it for and we'll ship it as soon as the return arrives. To get the exchange faster, simply place a new order and return the original order to us using our return label and we'll do a refund on the first order once it gets here. We highly suggest placing a new order right away to ensure stock availability. *Exchanges only available for US customers.

Be sure to send all requests for refunds to:

  • Solestruck
  • Attn: Returns Department
  • 10955 SW Commerce Circle, Suite A
  • Wilsonville, OR 97070

Questions? Instantly email us or call us at 1-800-494-1260 M-F 7am to 5pm PST.
